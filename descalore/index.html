Descalore

This project is about developing an expert system that focuses on young adults 4 who wants to diet and lose weight. So, developing this dietary expert system known as “DESCALORE” can help ease the user. It is a responsive website where users who want to diet can surf through their desktop or phone to create their diet journey. It will also enhance the plan for each user’s diet journey based on their details.
